About "We keep you out of troubled waters"

Founded in early 2013 across Marston Quad from a building sharing its name, Bridges Consulting is a strategy and management consulting firm unlike any other. At Bridges, we believe that building a strong working rapport with clients is pivotal to understanding the unique opportunities and challenges each faces in today's increasingly globalized market. Gained from years of industry experience, our firm's real-world expertise spans a wide range of industries, several of which are software, retail, and financial services. We leverage our human capital to deliver innovative solutions that meet client needs and consistently exceed client expectations. Our goal is to enable our clients to establish and maintain a competitive advantage as leaders in their respective industries. Our name "Bridges" embodies the different types and sizes of client companies as well as the firm's strategy expertise that links a company's past to a successful future. Successful client outcomes are built upon a strong corporate strategy, and our team at Bridges aims to be a solid foundation for partnering with clients to identify and to put into place effective business strategies.
